Arduino TFT shield v1.xx for Arduino UNO, Mega2560, ESP-32
3.5' 320x240 TFT with resistive touchscreen, 5 buttons, camera input connector

Shield Feature: 
To work with the Arduino shield, you must use a power adapter (with a voltage of at least 6 V and a current of at least 1A), which must be connected to the power port on the Arduino board.
